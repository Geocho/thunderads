Motion System

Move like Thunderads.

The motion layer should feel charged, not busy. Snap over drift. Pulse over gimmick. Every move needs a job: attention, transition, or emphasis.

Movement Principle

Fast entry. Clean hold. Sharp exit.

Hero pulses, cursor trails, line activations, and case transitions should all feel like one system — not random effects fighting each other.

Hero pulseSlow ambient energy from the center every 6–8 seconds. Low opacity. No constant flashing.
Hover responseButtons, cards, and cases react in under 180ms. Tiny lift. Tiny glow. No floaty delay.
Section revealUse vertical rise plus fade. 30–40px max. Stagger only when there is hierarchy to show.
Transition strikeUse the Motion Strike KV for reels, intros, and case breaks where speed matters more than explanation.
MicroCursor ring, button snap, card glow, title nudge.
MidSection pulse, KV hover, case-study reveal, nav state change.
MacroHero build, intro bumper, reel opener, cinematic case transition.

Thunderads is a creative strategy and production practice founded in Athens in 2013. We deliver brand platforms, campaigns, and storytelling systems for clients who want work that moves — not work that checks a box.

Founder George Chorevas brings over two decades of experience across advertising, film production, and brand strategy. From executive roles at McCann and Sohosquare to independent practice, the through-line has always been the same: ideas with ambition, execution without excuses.

We operate internationally and are open to projects from anywhere. The practice is built to be lean, fast, and fully committed — with AI-augmented workflows that compress timelines without compressing quality.
